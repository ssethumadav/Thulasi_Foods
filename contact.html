Our Story

A family tradition turned into a passion for authentic flavours

Born in a Traditional Kitchen

Thulasi Foods was born out of a simple desire — to share the authentic flavours of a South Indian home kitchen with every household. Our journey began years ago with a grandmother's recipes, passed down through generations.

Every product we make carries the warmth of home cooking. We wake up early, select fresh whole spices, and grind them with the same care and precision that has defined our family's cooking for decades.

We believe that real food needs no shortcuts. That's why everything we make is completely free of preservatives, artificial flavours, or chemicals of any kind.
